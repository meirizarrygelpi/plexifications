\chapter{Bi-Nilplex Numbers}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
An exo-2 real number has the form
\begin{equation}
    a_{0} + a_{1} W + a_{2} X + a_{3} WX
\end{equation}
These follow from a parabolic Cayley-Dickson construct on the exo-1 reals.

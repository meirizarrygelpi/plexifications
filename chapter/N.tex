\chapter{Dual Bi-Complex Numbers}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
An exo-1 quaternion has the form
\begin{equation}
    a_{0} + a_{1} A + a_{2} B + a_{3} AB + a_{4} W + a_{5} AW + a_{6} BW + a_{7} ABW
\end{equation}
These follow from a parabolic Cayley-Dickson construct on the quaternions.

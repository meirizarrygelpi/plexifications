\chapter{Hyper Complex Numbers}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
An exo-2 binion has the form
\begin{equation}
    a_{0} + a_{1} A + a_{2} W + a_{3} AW + a_{4} X + a_{5} AX + a_{6} WX + a_{7} AWX
\end{equation}
These follow from a parabolic Cayley-Dickson construct on the exo-1 binions.

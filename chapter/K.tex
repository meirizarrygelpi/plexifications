\chapter{Tri-Nilplex Numbers}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
An exo-3 real number has the form
\begin{equation}
    a_{0} + a_{1} W + a_{2} X + a_{3} WX + a_{4} Y + a_{5} WY + a_{6} XY + a_{7} WXY
\end{equation}
These follow from a parabolic Cayley-Dickson construct on the exo-2 reals.

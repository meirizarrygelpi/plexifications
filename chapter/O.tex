\begin{equation}
    a_{0} + a_{1} A + a_{2} S + a_{3} AS + a_{4} W + a_{5} AW + a_{6} SW + a_{7} ASW
\end{equation}
These follow from a parabolic Cayley-Dickson construct on the split quaternions.
